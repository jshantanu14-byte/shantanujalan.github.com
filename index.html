About Me

Finance professional with a global perspective

I'm a Financial Analyst at illumynt, where I work closely with the CFO on the numbers that shape how the business moves. My day-to-day covers everything from revenue and margin reporting to budgeting cycles and cost deep-dives, with the goal of making sure leadership always has reliable information when it matters most.

Finance has taken me far. I've studied and worked across India, Thailand, France and the United States, starting with a Siemens internship in Bangkok during my undergrad, building equity models in Jaipur, building financial data infrastructure at a fintech in Noida, and eventually completing a Master's degree with the first year at SKEMA Business School in Paris and the second year at North Carolina State University in Raleigh, NC, while leading a research project for UniCredit in New York.

I hold the CFA Charter and passed all three levels, scoring in the top 10% throughout the program. It took three years of focused effort alongside a full-time job, and it fundamentally shaped how I think about investment analysis, risk and valuation.
